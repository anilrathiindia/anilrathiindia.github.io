Practice Areas

Areas of Practice

All civil matters governed by the Civil Procedure Code, 1908 and applicable Indian laws before courts in Jodhpur.

01

Property & Title Suits

Declaration of title, recovery of possession, partition & cancellation of sale deeds.

 02

Civil Suits & Execution

Plaint to decree — trial, judgment & execution with attachment of property.

 03

Injunctions & Interim Relief

Temporary injunction, stay of proceedings & attachment before judgment.

 04

Succession & Inheritance

Probate, succession certificates, partition & will disputes across all communities.

 05

Contract & Recovery Suits

Breach of contract, money recovery & specific performance of agreements.

 06

Writ Petitions

Mandamus, Certiorari, Prohibition & Quo Warranto before Rajasthan High Court.

 07

Civil Appeals & Revisions

First appeal, second appeal & revision against subordinate court orders.

 08

Tenancy & Rent Disputes

Eviction, rent fixation & tenancy rights before Rent Tribunals and Civil Courts.

 09

Declaratory Suits

Declaration of legal rights, permanent injunction & rectification of instruments.

 10

Matrimonial Civil Matters

Maintenance, restitution of conjugal rights & spousal property disputes.

 11

Arbitration & Lok Adalat

Arbitral proceedings, award enforcement & ADR reference for civil disputes.

 12

Legal Notices & Drafting

Notices, plaints, affidavits, vakalatnamas & deeds as per court formats.
